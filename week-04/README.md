**Phase:** Dev Foundations | **Reward:** 100 TOURS

## Assignment
Learn .gitignore patterns, the .env.example workflow, and understand what happens when secrets leak. Study real examples of leaked keys and how to rotate compromised credentials.

## Deliverable

- .gitignore best practices
- What should NEVER be committed (private keys, .env, node_modules, mnemonics)
- Steps to take if a secret is accidentally committed
- GitHub's secret scanning alerts
